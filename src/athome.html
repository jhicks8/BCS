Best Community Services

Welcome

Community Center located next to Imaginary Park has been a cornerstone of our community for nearly 10 years. We offer a wide array of classes and sporting events for you and your family to enjoy. There is something for all ages! It also serves as a multipurpose space for community events, meetings, lectures, and activities! We look forward to seeing you soon.

Services

We offer a wide range of services: from clubs and classes to at-home nursing for the elderly. A community that learns and plays together, stays together! If you have a large event coming up and need a venue, our great hall is available for rental. Create a lasting memory of your wedding/bar mitsvah/communion/anything surronded by friends and family right here in the community center.

Donate

Our Community center is prodominately funded by donations from members of the community. We greatly appreciate any aid you feel comfortable providing. You can sign your name to be listed on our list of donors or can donate anonymously!

Volunteer

Most of our services are offered and run by community volunteers. We are always in need of another pair of helpful hands. No experience required, only a can-do attitude.

Have a special knowledge or expertese in something you'd like to share with the world? Contact one of our employee's to see if you're a good fit to teach a community center class or coach one of our many kid's sports teams!
